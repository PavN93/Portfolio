About me

My name is Pawel and I was born in a small town in Poland - Tarnow. That is where I spent most of my life and 5 years ago I have moved to England.


I graduated from high school in Tarnow with with a high proficiency in electronics. My background experience mostly includes working with CNC machines although I'm in the process of becoming a web developer, which became my long-term goal.


I decided to start studying web development because it became one of my hobbies and I wanted to combine a hobby with a profession. In my free time I also like playing sports, reading and listening about new technologies and engineering.


Throughout the course I learnt a lot of new technologies. HTML, CSS and JavaScript are just the foundation, besides them I also know jQuery, node.js, express.js, mySQL and more.

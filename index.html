About me

My name is Pawel and I was born in small town in Poland - Tarnow. That is where I spent most of my life and 5 years ago I have moved to England. I graduated from high school in Tarnow with electronic specialization. My background experience mostly includes working with CNC machines although I'm in process of becomming a web developer, which became my current goal for future career. I decided to take this path because computers and subject related to them are my hobby and I wanted to connect hobby with career. I also like to read and listen about technical/engineering solution used in the industry.
